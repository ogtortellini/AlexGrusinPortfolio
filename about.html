My Engineering Journey

My fascination with engineering began early, fueled by a desire to understand how things work and a drive to create innovative solutions. This passion led me to pursue Mechanical Engineering at Texas A&M University, where I've immersed myself in challenging coursework and hands-on projects.

I thrive on dissecting complex systems, identifying inefficiencies, and developing practical, robust improvements. Whether it's designing a custom component in CAD, simulating fluid dynamics, or troubleshooting a mechanical failure, I approach each challenge with a blend of analytical rigor and creative problem-solving.
