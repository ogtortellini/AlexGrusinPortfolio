Mars EDL Simulation

This simulation models the entry, descent, and landing of a Mars rover using realistic physics and control logic. It integrates Python numerical methods and aerospace principles to determine success conditions for soft landings.

  • Modeled terminal velocity, parachute dynamics, and retro-thrust
  • Used RK4 integration to simulate time-dependent descent
  • Evaluated landing success via speed ≤ 1.0 m/s and altitude ≥ 4.5 m
